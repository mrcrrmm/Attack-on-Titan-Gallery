Attack on Titan

About

Attack on Titan is a Japanese dark fantasy anime and manga series set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to giant humanoid creatures called Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they join the military to fight these Titans, uncover the secrets behind their existence, and confront the truth about their own society. The series is known for its intense action, deep political intrigue, and complex characters.
